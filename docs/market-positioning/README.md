# SuggaPlay Market Positioning

> **Source of truth for all marketing copy and homepage messaging.**
> Primary document: [`SuggaPlay-Brand-Positioning.pdf`](./SuggaPlay-Brand-Positioning.pdf) (from `new brand.pdf`)

## Brand descriptor

**Not a dating app.**

> A private members' club for modern relationships.

## Emotional tone

Luxury fashion campaign — not startup, not corporate, not LinkedIn.

References: Saint Laurent, Calvin Klein, Soho House, Jacquemus, holiday romance, summer in Ibiza, late nights in London, beautiful people, sexual confidence, creative freedom.

## Hero

**Headline:** Meet people who live life on their own terms.

**Sub copy:** For beautiful connections, honest intentions and unforgettable chemistry.

## The problem / club intro

SuggaPlay is a curated members' club for ambitious, attractive and open-minded people. Whether you're looking for love, **lust**, luxury or something in between, you'll meet people who are honest about what they're looking for from the very beginning.

- Dating shouldn't feel like guesswork.
- The best relationships begin with honesty.

**Honesty manifesto:** Most dating apps expect people to pretend they're all looking for the same thing. They're not. Some people want love. Some want adventure. Some want luxury. Some simply want extraordinary chemistry. SuggaPlay believes honesty is more attractive than pretending.

## Our solution

**Built around honesty.**

Attraction shouldn't be complicated. SuggaPlay gives every member the freedom to express what they're genuinely looking for before the first conversation begins.

## Relationship preferences (hero feature)

Tell people what you're looking for. Not what dating apps think you should be looking for. Choose one or more.

❤ Long-term relationship · ✨ Casual dating · 🥂 Luxury lifestyle · 🤝 Mutually beneficial · ✈ Travel partner · 🎨 Creative connection · 🌍 Open relationship · 🌈 Ethical non-monogamy · 👀 Exploring

## Membership

One membership. Unlimited conversations. No credits. No tokens. No counting every message.

Simple. Luxury brands don't oversell.

## We believe… (manifesto)

- Life is too short for mixed signals.
- Chemistry matters.
- Honesty is attractive.
- Curiosity is beautiful.
- Privacy is essential.
- Everyone deserves respect.
- Adults should be free to define their own relationships.

## How it works

1. **Express yourself.** Choose your intentions before you meet anyone.
2. **Find your people.** Meet members whose expectations match yours.
3. **Enjoy the connection.** No games. No guessing. No pretending.

## Permission (core brand opportunity)

SuggaPlay should sell **permission** — not exclusivity (Raya) or belonging alone (Soho House).

- Permission to stop pretending.
- Permission to be honest.
- Permission to admit you want: Love. Sex. Adventure. Luxury. A travel companion. A mentor. A benefactor. A creative muse. Or simply an unforgettable summer.

No dating brand really owns that space. SuggaPlay could.

## Tone of voice

Confident · Premium · Welcoming · Discreet · Modern · Sophisticated · Honest about desire

Never sensational, exploitative, or misleading — but do not avoid words like *lust* when they serve honesty.
